First Aid Box Details

Item Details

Cotton

Soft, absorbent cotton used to clean wounds, apply antiseptics, and stop minor bleeding.

Bandage Roll

A strip of cloth used to cover wounds, hold dressings in place, and support injured body parts.

Adhesive Bandage

A small self-adhesive bandage used to cover minor cuts, scrapes, and wounds to protect them from dirt and germs.

Dettol

An antiseptic solution used to clean wounds and help prevent infection by killing germs.

Betadine

A povidone-iodine antiseptic used to disinfect cuts, burns, and wounds before dressing them.

Oral Rehydration Salts

A mixture of salts and glucose dissolved in water to replace fluids and minerals lost due to diarrhea, vomiting, or dehydration.

Pain Relief Gel

A topical gel applied to the skin to relieve muscle pain, joint pain, sprains, strains, and swelling by reducing pain and providing a cooling effect.
